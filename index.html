Our Insurance Solutions

Capital Providers offers a wide range of commercial insurance solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of your business. Below are some of our key services designed to protect your business operations.

Workers Compensation

Protects businesses and employees by covering medical expenses and lost wages due to workplace injuries.

General Liability Insurance

Shields businesses from claims related to bodily injury, property damage, and legal costs arising from accidents.

Commercial Auto Insurance

Provides coverage for vehicles used for business purposes, including liability and physical damage protection.

Umbrella Insurance

Offers extra liability coverage beyond the limits of standard policies, ensuring additional protection.

Professional Liability Insurance

Covers claims related to errors, omissions, or negligence in professional services.

Employment Practices Liability

Protects against claims of wrongful termination, discrimination, or harassment in the workplace.

Cyber Liability Insurance

Provides coverage for data breaches, cyberattacks, and other digital security threats.

Group Health Insurance

Offers health coverage plans for employees, enhancing benefits and employee retention.

Surety & Fidelity Bonds

Guarantees contract performance and protects against employee theft or fraud.
